Project Document: Self-Evolving AI Trading Venture
1. Core Vision
The objective is to develop a fully autonomous, self-evolving AI system designed for stock trading. This system is intended to reason, perform tasks, and evolve its own logic to maintain a superior edge over existing algorithms. The stock trading mission serves as the primary proof-of-concept for this broader autonomous architecture.
2. Competitive Advantage
 * Advanced Mathematical Foundation: Utilizing a PhD-level background in mathematics to design complex algorithms and optimization models that standard engineering approaches (CS-only) may lack.
 * Architectural Superiority: Focus on self-evolution and reasoning capabilities rather than static model training.
 * Strategic Agility: Targeting specific niches and market inefficiencies where a specialized, high-speed startup can outperform larger, slower-moving financial institutions.
3. Business & Funding Model
 * Capital Strategy: Transitioning away from trading personal capital to a service-fee model. The goal is to attract and manage outside capital, generating revenue through performance or management fees.
 * Exit & Growth: Using the system’s proven effectiveness to secure external funding and scale the team.
Strategic Roadmap
Phase I: Market Research & Niche Identification
 * Identify specific trading pairs or market conditions (e.g., high volatility, specific sectors) where mathematical complexity provides a barrier to entry.
 * Analyze the limitations of current institutional tools in these specific niches.
Phase II: Proof of Concept (PoC)
 * Develop a demonstration of the self-evolving mechanism.
 * Backtest the mathematical models to generate concrete effectiveness data.
Phase III: Pitching & Fundraising
 * Build a technical pitch deck highlighting the "Math PhD" advantage and the self-evolving nature of the AI.
 * Define the milestone goals to reassure investors of the system's reliability and scalability.
Phase IV: Scaling & Team Building
 * Acquire the necessary hardware and high-level talent to expand the system's capabilities beyond the initial trading mission.
Condensed Summary
 * Current Goal: Develop a self-evolving, autonomous AI trading system.
